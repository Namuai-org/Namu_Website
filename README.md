# Namu

Namu builds AI systems designed from the ground up for African communities, in their own languages and cultural contexts. We are not adapting existing tools or adding a translation layer. We are building new systems, ones that are shaped around how people actually speak, think, and live.

Namu  is an AI venture, but our real differentiator is cultural depth and linguistic understanding. We are building for one of the most underserved markets in the world, and we are doing it as people who come from that market.
